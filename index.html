About Me

Mechan­ical Engineer & Builder

I'm a second-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Calgary's Schulich School of Engineering, graduating April 2028.

I'm an active member of the UCalgary FSAE Racing Team, where I work on chassis and controls — designing safety-critical components, producing manufacturing drawings, and seeing them through to fabrication. I enjoy working at the intersection of design, fabrication, and problem-solving.

Outside of FSAE, I've worked on autonomous robotics, embedded sensor systems, and data science projects — always looking for opportunities to apply engineering thinking to new problems.
